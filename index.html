Beware, beware, the town of Oswego!

This website is the culminating project for our ISC 325: Hypermedia class offered this semester Spring 2019 at SUNY Oswego. The class tasked us with emerging from our humble abode on campus into the town of Oswego. Giving the students the criteria of designing a multimedia project on one aspect of the city of Oswego. Our group and its members have such decided to take you on a Haunted Tour of Oswego. Focusing on a few of the many haunted sites in Oswego and displaying their historical accounts and stories of the paranormal. Helping to assist in creating a more fluid integration between the work of college students and the connected town of Oswego.

Enter, if you dare!
